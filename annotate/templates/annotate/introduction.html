Introduction

Welcome to HHUD frame annotation system. The purpose of this system is to facilitate, in an intuitive way, the annotatation process of words and phrases in terms of semantic frames, i.e., a specialized format for organizing and storing data about events and situations, including their participants.

To use the system, or for questions, please contact Regina Stodden or Behrang Qasemizadeh.
